Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Lavaado 5-in-1 Wassheets

How do Lavaado wassheets work?

Our innovative wassheets dissolve completely in water, releasing powerful cleaning agents, fabric softener, color protectant, and fresh scent. Each sheet contains a precisely measured dose for optimal cleaning performance with no waste.

How many sheets should I use per load?

For standard loads, use just one sheet. For heavily soiled clothes or larger loads (over 5kg), you can use two sheets for extra cleaning power.

Are Lavaado wassheets safe for sensitive skin?

Yes! Our wassheets are hypoallergenic, free from harsh chemicals, and dermatologically tested. The Neutral option is specifically formulated for sensitive skin and is free from added fragrances.

How long will one package last?

One wassheet is good for 2-3 loads of laundry, depending on load size and soil level. Our 40-sheet package typically lasts for about 100 washes, which is approximately 3-4 months for an average household.

Can I use Lavaado wassheets in all washing machines?

Absolutely! Our wassheets work perfectly in all types of washing machines - front-loaders, top-loaders, and HE machines. They're also safe for septic systems and can be used in cold, warm, or hot water.
